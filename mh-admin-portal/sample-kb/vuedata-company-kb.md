## Enterprise Products

### Go-BOSS
Go-BOSS lets you build your own streaming service, enabling you to manage, publish, and monetize your content. You can release custom branded apps to multiple platforms and app stores. You own the content experience, and Go-BOSS owns the platform. Ideal for media companies and content creators looking to launch their own OTT service.

### DB-DaaS (Database as a Service)
DB-DaaS is a robust, scalable platform for optimizing database release automation. It integrates easily with existing CI/CD environments, aligning with current development processes and guidelines. DB-DaaS streamlines database deployments and reduces manual intervention in release cycles.

### Perchat.ai
Perchat.ai is an AI-powered conversational platform that enables businesses to automate customer interactions across channels. It delivers instant, contextual, and human-like support at scale. Suitable for customer service automation, lead qualification, and internal helpdesk operations.

### ShipKasa
ShipKasa is a scalable shipping label software designed to simplify fulfillment workflows, optimize shipping costs, and support business growth through faster, more efficient shipping operations. Ideal for e-commerce businesses and logistics companies.
